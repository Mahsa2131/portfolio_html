Hello, my name is Mahsa.

I recently graduated from Linköping University, where I focused on UX design. My background is a bit of a mix—I’ve done everything from jewelry design to consulting and teaching. It’s given me a different way of looking at design, always thinking about how to make things better for the people using them.

I’m the kind of person who’s always thinking of new ideas. Whether I’m working on a project or just going about my day, I love coming up with creative ways to solve problems or make things easier. And I really pay attention to the details—it’s part of what drives me in my work.

Outside of design, I’m all about nature and animals, which is why I’ve chosen to be vegetarian. Traveling is a big inspiration for me too; it helps me see the world (and design) from fresh perspectives. When I’m not working, I’m usually watching movies, reading, or just enjoying a bit of downtime.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio! Feel free to explore my work, and if you’d like to collaborate, I’d love to hear from you.
